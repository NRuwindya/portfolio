Edufix

Offers personalized career paths and courses


Introduction:

In this project, I developed a user-friendly web application to assist students and graduates in making informed decisions about their academic paths and careers. The platform delivers personalized course recommendations tailored to users' hobbies, job interests, and educational goals, enhancing their journey towards educational and career success.

Problem overview:

  • Students and graduates often face challenges in navigating the complex landscape of education and career planning. The abundance of information coupled with a lack of personalized guidance leads to confusion and uncertainty. Key challenges include:
    • Difficulty in identifying passions and skills relevant to career prospects.
    • Overwhelm in making prudent choices about further education due to an array of options.
    • Traditional education systems falling short in imparting career-ready skills.
  • Inadequate support in adapting to an evolving job market.

    • Proposed Solution:

  • The solution is a web application equipped with AI-powered chatbots to assist users in finding personalized courses and career pathways efficiently.
  • Machine learning algorithms analyzing user inputs to match them with relevant courses.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) scanning user text to understand interests and personality traits.
  • Personalized course recommendations based on user inputs and identified personality traits.
  • Assistance in making the best use of transition periods between educational levels.

    • UI/UX Design Process

    The UI/UX design process for the personalized course recommendation web application followed a structured approach tailored to meet the needs of students and graduates effectively. Here's a simplified breakdown of the key stages:

  • User Research and Analysis: We started by gathering insights through surveys, interviews, and usability tests to understand user challenges and expectations.
  • Ideation and Conceptualization: Brainstormed concepts and created user personas to visualize how different users would interact with the platform.
  • Wireframing and Information Architecture: Developed low-fidelity wireframes to outline the layout and navigation, ensuring clarity and simplicity.
  • Prototyping and Iterative Design: Built interactive prototypes and refined them based on user feedback, focusing on usability improvements.
  • Visual Design and UI Styling: Defined the color scheme, typography, and visual elements to create a cohesive and appealing interface.
  • Usability Testing and Feedback Incorporation: Conducted usability tests with real users and integrated feedback to enhance functionality and user experience.
  • Finalization and Handoff: Compiled design specifications and assets for seamless implementation by the development team.
