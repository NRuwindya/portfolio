Foodiespot

Food recipe providing mobile app

Project Overview:

Foodiespot is a food recipe providing app developed to address common challenges faced by individuals with limited cooking knowledge and busy schedules. The app aims to simplify meal planning, promote healthier eating practices, and offer resources for quick and simple meal ideas.

Problem Statement:

  • Limited cooking knowledge leading to last-minute, unhealthy, or repetitive food choices.
  • Unfamiliarity with ingredients affecting meal diversity.
  • Challenges in meal planning due to busy schedules.

    • User Needs and Requirements Based on User Research

  • Users need a simple and easy-to-use interface for meal planning.
  • Instructional support to improve cooking abilities.
  • Preference for healthier recipes with nutritional information.
